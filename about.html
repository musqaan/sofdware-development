About Mapro

Passion for Quality, Taste, and Purity

Our Story

Mapro is a trusted brand in the food and beverage industry, offering high-quality fruit-based products like jams, syrups, juices, and more. Established in the 1970s, we have grown from a small family business to one of the leading names in the market, loved by people of all ages.

At Mapro, we believe in delivering products that are natural, fresh, and bursting with flavor. We carefully source the best fruits and ingredients to bring you the finest products that can be enjoyed in every home. Our commitment to quality ensures that our products maintain their authentic taste, whether you're spreading jam on your toast or making a refreshing drink.
